Research and analysis of **performance bottlenecks in large-scale agent-based simulations (ABM)**, presented as a poster at **ACACES 2026** (HiPEAC Summer School, Fiuggi, Italy) with a merit-based grant.

## Approach

- Apply the **DAMOV** methodology to identify memory, compute, and I/O bottlenecks
- Run experiments on **Google Cloud VMs** to study scalability limits
- Connect findings to hardware-software co-design opportunities for HPC simulation frameworks

## Related frameworks

- BioDynaMo
- CARTopiaX
- NeuroDev

This project bridges agent-based modeling research with computer architecture and performance engineering.
